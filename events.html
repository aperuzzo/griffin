July 7 2015

Introducing Brent Foreman!

We are proud to introduce a new artist for the gallery, Brent Foreman. Brent was born and raised in Minneapolis, Minnesota. He attended Minnesota State University in Mankato where he completed his Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Fine Art.

His recent work is reminiscent of such painters as Gottlieb, Motherwell and Rauschenberg. It is defined by large areas of whimsical color, unconventional shapes and a loose brush stroke. He has an innate ability to combine textures, colors and shape in each and every piece.
