GRIFFIN GALLERY

Visit Minneapolis and St Paul’s premier art gallery

About

Griffin Gallery, in the historic Camelot Castle, is dedicated to the exhibition of representational art, forever classic and enduring. Our collection, one of the largest in the Minneapolis metro, features traditional and contemporary fine art originals, limited edition prints, etchings, sculpture, celebrity artifacts, sports memorabilia and more. The gallery features both national and internationally acclaimed artists as well as the best of today’s emerging artists from the Minneapolis area and beyond.

Griffin Gallery hosts a series of one-person exhibitions as well as seminars throughout the year featuring new works by all the gallery artists. Griffin Gallery prides itself on providing the highest standard of quality and integrity to the client.

See the largest display of internationally known artist, Bill Mack’s relief sculptures, drawings, paintings, cast bronzes and the legendary Hollywood sign project.
