Anne Packard

The painting tradition is strong in Packard’s family from her grandfather, Max Bohm, turn of the century impressionist, to her grandmother, great aunt, uncle, mother, and daughter.

Today, Packard is the most renowned artist in the Provincetown Art Colony. Her name has become one of the most recognized in the Boston art circles.

In this gallery we feature a selection of Anne's works. More are available to view on request. Please contact us with any inquiries.
